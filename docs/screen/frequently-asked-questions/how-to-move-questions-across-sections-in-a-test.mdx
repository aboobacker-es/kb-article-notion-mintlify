You can organize the Question in your test under logical categories called sections. You can create multiple sections within a Test and use the **Move** option to organize the Questions in different sections.

1. Click **Tests** on the home page, select the required Test.
1. In the Questions tab, add Questions to the Test from the Library.
1. To create a section, click the plus sign and select **Create section**.

**Section 1** is added, and by default, all the Test Questions now belong to this section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-to-move-questions-across-sections-in-a-test/1b4610434e2e4cd0.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

*Questions added to Section 1*

1. Click **Add another section** to add additional sections.
1. Select Question(s) from **Section 1** and click **Move**. From the list, select the alternate section to move the Question.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-to-move-questions-across-sections-in-a-test/ddee6699a941537f.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

*Moving the Questions to another section*

The Question is moved from **Section 1** to the selected section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-to-move-questions-across-sections-in-a-test/33a00685f8c42dfc.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

*Question moved to another section*

1. In a similar manner, select the required Questions from any section and click **Move** to move them to an alternate section.
1. In a scenario where the Test Questions are not added to any section, they are listed under the "**Unassigned Questions**" category. You can select the Questions from this list and click **Move** to move them to the relevant Sections.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-to-move-questions-across-sections-in-a-test/464d6b10e8acabd4.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

*Moving Questions from the unassigned category*

**Note**: It is recommended that you use the **Move** option and avoid the 'drag-and-drop method to move Questions from one section to another.
