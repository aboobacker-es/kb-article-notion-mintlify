HackerRank for Work allows Recruiters to perform a trial run of their Tests before inviting candidates. You can validate each Test Question during the trial by submitting the answer and viewing the results.

### Steps to Try a Question

- 1. Click **Tests** on the home page and select a Test.
- 2. On the Questions page of the Test, hover your mouse over the Question you want to validate.
- 3. In the **Actions** column, click on three dots and select **Try question**.

### Steps to Try a Test

- To validate a test, click Try Test on the Questions page to perform a trial run and attempt all the questions. You will be able to log in, view, and solve Questions in a manner similar to how your candidates will attempt this Test.
- You can share the test with other recruiters on your team and have them validate the test questions. For more information, refer to the [Sharing a Test for Benchmarking](https://support.hackerrank.com/hc/en-us/articles/115006004787-Sharing-a-test-for-benchmarking) topic.

**Note**: During your test trials, sometimes, when you try to log in to a test multiple times, the browser-level cache may prevent you from retrying the test. You should access the Test in a new browser window to overcome this issue.
