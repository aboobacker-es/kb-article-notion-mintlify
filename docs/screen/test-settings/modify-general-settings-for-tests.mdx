HackerRank allows you to modify the general settings of a test such as key metadata and scheduling options. Most of these settings are defined when you initially create the test.

### Prerequisites

You must have already created a test.

### Modifying general settings

To modify the general settings of a test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to update.
1. Go to **Settings \> General.**
