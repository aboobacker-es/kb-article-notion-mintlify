You can enhance test integrity by hiding question titles from candidates during an assessment. When you enable this feature, candidates cannot view the original titles of questions in a test. This reduces the risk of online answer searches.

**Note: **The Hide Question Title feature is disabled by default. You must manually enable it for each test.

## Enabling Hide Question Title

To enable Hide Question Title:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to hide the question title.
1. Go to** Settings \> Questions.**
1. Turn on the **Hide Question Title **toggle.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/hide-question-title/815e347088c8405e.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Save Changes**.

**Note:** The changes apply to all questions in the test.

## Candidate experience

When you hide question titles:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hide-question-title/4f4e8fea9cd16b62.png" alt="image" />
</Frame>

- Candidates do not see the original titles for any questions.
- Questions appear with generic labels such as Question 1, Question 2, and so on.
