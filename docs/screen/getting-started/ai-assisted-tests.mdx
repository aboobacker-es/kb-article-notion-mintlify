This feature is part of the AI Add-on. For more information, see [📄 HackerRank AI Add-on](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/8704fb40-5ded-4188-b5a6-cfd653d0f5c9).

HackerRank enables AI assistance for project-based coding questions to mirror real-world development environments. This feature helps candidates experience realistic workflows and allows recruiters to evaluate practical problem-solving more accurately.

The AI Assistant operates in the following modes:

- **Unguarded**: Allows candidates to interact freely with the AI, similar to real-world AI coding tools and agent-style workflows.
- **Guarded (Default)**: Provides help with syntax, platform navigation, and conceptual guidance but does not generate complete solutions.

<Note>
**Note:** Contact your account manager to change the default setting or to learn more about the available modes.
</Note>

The AI Assistant currently supports the following question types:

- Coding
- Database
- Data Science
- Projects

### Enable AI Assistant for Tests

To enable the AI Assistant:

#### Step 1: Enable AI Assistant in Company Settings

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to **Settings**.
1. Select** Test Settings** from the left panel under the **Company** section.
1. In the **Test Evaluation** section, turn on the **AI Assistance for Candidates** toggle.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/ai-assisted-tests/e57c5be1696b6368.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Save Changes**.

#### Step 2: Set up AI Assistant at the section level

1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test for which you want to add the AI assistant.
1. Hover over the section name and select the **Settings** icon.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/ai-assisted-tests/07850d243912e71e.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the **Section Settings **dialog, select the AI assistance level you want to enable for candidates:

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/ai-assisted-tests/a0444687c4f77191.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  <!-- Unhandled Notion block: bulleted_list_item -->

  <!-- Unhandled Notion block: bulleted_list_item -->

  <!-- Unhandled Notion block: bulleted_list_item -->
1. Click **Save**.

### Report insights

After the test is complete, the candidate report displays AI-specific interaction data.

- **AI Assistant Transcript:** This transcript shows the full conversation between the candidate and the AI assistant in the **Detailed Report**. It is enabled by default for all AI-Assisted Tests.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/ai-assisted-tests/6fd14b80989cb712.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
- **AI Fluency** (optional): Provides recruiters with a holistic view of how candidates interact with the AI assistant during a test. For more information, see [📄 AI Fluency](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/5075d153-4ed1-4bf5-bea2-8fbdd0a4643f).

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<Accordion title="**Does the AI-Assisted Summary work in proctored tests?**">
Yes. In proctored tests, the **Performance Summary** appears in the new reports interface.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**Can I view the AI Fluency in older reports?**">
In the classic reports view, the AI Fluency is available only at the question level, but not at the test level.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**Is the AI Fluency reliable?**">
Refer to the [📄 AI Fluency](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/5075d153-4ed1-4bf5-bea2-8fbdd0a4643f) article.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="**When should I use the Unguarded AI Assistant?**">
You can use the Unguarded AI Assistant when you want to evaluate how candidates work with AI in real-world, agent-style workflows.
</Accordion>
