### What is a Certified Assessment?

HackerRank Certified Assessments are pre-built role-specific tests created after long-term research and analysis of the questions that match the industry standards for the given role and assure a quality outcome from the screening process.

These Certified Assessments follow industry norms and design a universal test for a specific role. Any company that wants to hire for that role can use this single test to assess its candidates. This provides an unbiased interview experience for both the companies and the candidates.

### Benefits

- **100% HackerRank Managed Screening:** HackerRank entirely manages these assessments. You do not have to depend upon your recruiting and engineering teams to create the test questions or manage the test.
- **Unbiased Assessments: **HackerRank uses a third-party content bias checker that helps to provide an unbiased assessment experience.
- **Leakage Protected Skill Bundles: **You will not be required to keep a tab on your tests to check if a question is leaked. HackerRank automatically detects and removes the leaked questions and replaces them with new questions. The leak detection process runs daily, and questions are flagged immediately once detected during the daily run.
- **Robust Plagiarism Detection: **HackerRank provides the market's most powerful plagiarism detection software to detect malpractice.
- **Accurate Classification: **Our team has built a machine-learning-powered role mapping engine that conducts in-depth research by scanning thousands of job descriptions throughout the industry. This approach helps us accurately list the skills required for a specific job role.
- **No Repetition:** HackerRank follows a 10-step robust evaluation process to select questions. The possibility of two developers getting the same set of questions is 1 in 1000.

### Creating a Certified Assessment

- Click on the **Create Test** option and select the role for which you want to assess the candidates.
- Search for the desired role or the skill you want to assess. If the role you selected has a Certified Assessment you can see the badge as shown in the below picture.
- A Certified Assessment is categorized into skill-based sections. You can see the skills mapped to the role you have selected. You can add or remove these roles in the next step.

### Section Level Settings

#### Shuffling Sections

You can rearrange the order of skill bundles within a test. To reorder the skill bundles, hover your mouse cursor over a skill bundle, and while keeping the mouse button pressed, drag the skill bundle upwards or downwards to the desired position. This allows you to easily shuffle and reorder the sequence of skill bundles per your preference within the test.
