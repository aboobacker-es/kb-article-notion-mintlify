Archiving a test helps you manage your test library by moving unused tests out of the **Active Tests** tab. An archived test is no longer available for inviting candidates or publishing. You can restore an archived test at any time.

## Prerequisites

You must have an existing test in your HackerRank account.

## Archiving a test

To archive a test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to archive.
1. Click the more options menu (⋮) in the top right corner.
1. Select **Archive**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/archive-a-test/1d8c7bf68762e10e.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Archive** to confirm. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/archive-a-test/919aa236e8054cbd.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The test moves to the **Archived Tests** list.

## Restoring an archived test

To restore an archived test:

1. Open the test you want to restore.
1. Click the more options menu (⋮) in the top right corner.
1. Select **Restore**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/archive-a-test/a26789aa68b92e8a.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Restore **to confirm. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/archive-a-test/4b39f5e2662432e3.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

Once restored:

- The test moves back to your list of Active Tests.
- Candidate invites are re-enabled.
