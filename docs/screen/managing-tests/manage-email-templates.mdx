Email templates in HackerRank allow you to standardize and streamline candidate communications. You can create, edit, reuse, and share customized email invitation templates with your team to ensure consistent messaging across hiring scenarios.

For example, create one email template for campus hiring and another for lateral hiring. Save each template separately and select the appropriate one when you send invitations.

## Prerequisite

You must have a published test in your HackerRank for Work account.

## Creating a custom email template

To create a custom email template:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open a test.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/a7b30bf3fc768c5a.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click the **Templates** icon.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/5b253ed4f5986804.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **+Create New**.
1. Enter a template name and add content in the right pane. Use the formatting options to customize the content.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/f31f4c4b088059df.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. (Optional) Click **Add subject **to add a subject line for the template. The subject appears in all email invites that use this template. You can also include emojis in the subject line, if needed.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/da8d55dae2e1c92e.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Save**. The system saves your templates under **My Templates**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/2e6d20436c2d760c.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

## Editing a custom email template

To edit a custom email template:

1. Open a test.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.
1. Click the **Templates** icon.
1. Go to **My Templates**. This section lists templates you created.
1. Select the template you want to edit.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/98276f97711445db.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Make the necessary changes.
1. Click **Save**.

## Using a custom email template

To use a custom email template that you have created:

1. Open the test for which you want to use the template.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.
1. Click the **Templates** icon.
1. Go to **My Templates**. This section lists templates you created.
1. Select the template you want to use.
1. Click **Use this Template**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/ad44bc314729ce0b.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The label **In use** appears next to the template currently applied to the invite.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/49314d60137a87c0.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

<Note>
**Note:** To configure an invite email at the test level, see [📄 Configure Email Settings for Tests](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/bf7f1631-0ff2-4263-a607-82e422a2e381).
</Note>

## Using a shared email template

To use a shared email template:

1. Open the test for which you want to use the template.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.
1. Click the **Templates** icon.
1. Go to **Shared Templates**. This section lists templates created by other recruiters in your company.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/1a9f5c31e809f08c.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Select the template you want to use.
1. Click **Use this Template**. The selected template appears in the test invite.

## Setting a default email template

You can set one of your templates as the default for all email invites. When you set a default template, the email editor on the **Send Test Invites** page automatically loads it when you click **Invite** for a test

<Note>
**Note: **You can only set the templates you created as default templates.
</Note>

To set a default template:

1. Open a test.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.
1. Click the **Templates** icon.
1. Go to **My Templates**.
1. Click the star icon next to the template name. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/e35ac6ca5e14d936.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

## Deleting a custom email template

You can delete an email template if it is no longer in use.

<Note>
**Note:** You can only delete templates you created.
</Note>

To delete a custom email template:

1. Open a test.
1. Click **Invite** in the upper-right corner to open the **Send Test Invites** page.
1. Click the **Templates** icon.
1. Go to **My Templates**.
1. Select the template you want to delete.
1. Click the **Delete Template** icon. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/manage-email-templates/fcbb8d0ab433b633.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
