If you want a candidate to reattempt a test, you can delete the candidate’s test submission. This action permanently removes the candidate’s previous attempt, including all proctoring records, and resets their test status to **Invited**.

<Note>
**Note:** It is recommended that you do not delete a candidate test submitted if the test is in progress.
</Note>

## Prerequisites

You must have a test submitted by at least one candidate in your HackerRank for Work account.

## Deleting a test submission

To delete a test submission:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test that contains the submission you want to delete.
1. Go to the **Candidates** tab.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-a-test-submission/816eafe89bb51337.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Select the checkbox next to the candidate(s) whose submission you want to delete.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-a-test-submission/827a22fc61dbaa3c.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

<Note>
**Note:** You can also search for the candidate by their name or email if required.
</Note>

1. Click on **More \> Delete**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-a-test-submission/495e6efa750b6110.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Confirm**. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-a-test-submission/3009420e0f5e6935.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

<Note>
**Note: **Once you delete a candidate’s submission, the candidate can either retake the test using their original invitation link or you can send a new invitation. For more information, see [📄 Reinviting Candidates to a Test](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/a7b4d9b2-f6b2-4b57-826b-30061839be41).
</Note>
