Cloning a test allows you to create a new test with the same questions and settings as the original. Changes made to the original test after cloning do not appear in the cloned test, and changes in the cloned test do not affect the original.

After cloning, you can modify the cloned test by adding or removing questions or updating the test settings.

<Note>
**Note:**
</Note>

## Prerequisites

- You must have an existing test in your HackerRank account.

## Cloning a test

To clone a test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to clone.
1. Click the more options menu (⋮) in the top right corner.
1. Select **Clone**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/clone-a-test/9cae11b29d93b32e.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Clone** to confirm. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/clone-a-test/a1ff7ed542aab271.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click the **Test Created. Click here to go to the test** button to open the cloned test.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/clone-a-test/a95c18c965e40347.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

<Note>
**Note**: The cloned test name follows this format: **Original Test Name (Duplicate)**
</Note>

The cloned test appears on your **Tests** page. You can edit it as needed. To make the test available to candidates, click **Publish**.
