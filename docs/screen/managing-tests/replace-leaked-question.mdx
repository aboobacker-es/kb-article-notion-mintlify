When a question is flagged as leaked, a warning icon appears beside its title in your test. You can manually replace any question identified as leaked online.

When you click the warning icon, the following options appear:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/replace-leaked-question/fc58acf7de1fd802.png" alt="image" />
</Frame>

- **Subscribe to email alert**: Receive notifications when HackerRank detects a leaked test question. For more information, see [Configure Leakage Alert Emails](https://support.hackerrank.com/articles/2564162886-modifying-email-settings-for-tests#configure-leakage-alert-emails-15).
- **View leaked sources**: Review the online sources where the question appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/replace-leaked-question/8b1a2fa2280d8c58.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
- **Replace**: Replace the leaked question with a suitable alternative from the Library.

## Replacing a leaked question

To replace a leaked question:

1. Click the warning icon next to the leaked question.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/replace-leaked-question/687f1ff54e07c402.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Select **Replace**. The Library opens with filters applied based on the original question’s type, skill, and difficulty.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/replace-leaked-question/eeeb4c01651be9dc.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Choose a suitable replacement question and click **Replace**.
1. Compare the existing and new questions in the confirmation modal (name, skill, type, score, and duration).

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/replace-leaked-question/cfc8f042884bc108.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Confirm**.
1. Click **Publish** to make the updated test available to candidates.
