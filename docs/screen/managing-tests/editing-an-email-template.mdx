Once you are done creating the Email invite templates, you can edit your existing email invite Templates to make necessary changes. This article walks you through the steps to edit the existing email invite templates.

### Steps to Edit an Email Invite Template

**Prerequisites**

1. You must have created an email invite Template that you want to edit.

**Steps**

1. Navigate to the **Tests**. For the selected Test, click on the **Invite** icon on the extreme right.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/editing-an-email-template/705746312f958d2d.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

2. The **Send Test Invites** page is displayed. If you have set a default email Template, the email editor automatically populates the editor with the default Template format.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/editing-an-email-template/8927a6086d1d1a57.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

Refer to the [Using a Custom Email Invite Template](https://kb.hackerrank.com/articles/6675460135-using-a-custom-email-invite-template) article for more information.

3. In the editor, make necessary changes to the email invite. Click on the **Save Templates **icon. The Template window is displayed. The changes made to the email invite are shown.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/editing-an-email-template/caae00b35e5a765c.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

4. Click on the **Save **button. The Template is updated successfully.

Alternatively, you can also edit a template from the Template window.

1. Click on the **Templates **icon. The Templates window displays. Templates created by you are listed under **My Template**, and the ones created by other Recruiters in your HackerRank Team are listed under the **Shared Templates**.

2. Select the Template you want to edit. In the right pane, make necessary changes.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/editing-an-email-template/c15322f4d1e2b209.bin" alt="image" />
</Frame>

3. Click on the **Save **button. The Template is updated successfully.
