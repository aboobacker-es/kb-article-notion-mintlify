You can export test-level data as a CSV file that includes test details, test settings, attempt statistics, and more.

## Prerequisites

You must have existing test(s) in your HackerRank account.

## Exporting tests data

To export tests data:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. (Optional) Refine the list by applying filters in the left pane. You can filter by Labels, Owner, Role, Work Experience, Created At, and Certified Assessments.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/export-tests-data/3b85b051ab431a8c.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

<Note>
**Note:** The export includes only the data visible based on your selected filters.
</Note>

1. Click **Export**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/export-tests-data/d54db48728661ebb.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The platform automatically generates and downloads the CSV file. If the download does not start, select **Click here** in the confirmation dialog.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/export-tests-data/4b7cef7b7718fca9.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
