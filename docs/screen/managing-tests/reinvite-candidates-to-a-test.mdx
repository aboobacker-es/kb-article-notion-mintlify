If a candidate experiences an issue such as a power outage or loss of internet connection, you can reinvite them to retake the test at any time. You do not need to delete the previous invite or attempt before sending a new one.

**Note: **When you reinvite a candidate, their previous report is automatically deleted. To recover a deleted report, contact support@hackerrank.com.

## Prerequisites

- The candidate must have already been invited to or attempted the test.
- You must have a Recruiter license to reinvite candidates.

## Reinviting candidates to a test

To reinvite a candidate to a test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test from which you want to reinvite candidates.
1. Go to the **Candidates** tab.
1. Select the checkbox next to the candidate you want to reinvite.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/reinvite-candidates-to-a-test/94c2fa2a71cdfb76.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Invite selected**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/reinvite-candidates-to-a-test/19c08b2f16460010.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the **Send Test Invites **section,** **click **Send invite**.

  A confirmation message appears indicating that the candidate has already attempted the test.
1. Click **Re-invite** to send the test invitation again.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/reinvite-candidates-to-a-test/7de51fde7433e80b.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Send invite** to complete the reinvitation process.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/reinvite-candidates-to-a-test/167ec197bc22cb4f.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
