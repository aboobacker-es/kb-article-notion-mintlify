You can define a start and end time (including time zone) to control when a test remains active.

Candidates can attempt the test only during the active period. After the test expires:

- Invited candidates cannot access the test.
- The **Invite** button is disabled.
- You cannot invite new candidates.

## Prerequisite

You must have created a test.

## Modifying test expiration time

To modify the test expiration time:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to update.
1. Go to **Settings \> General**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/modify-test-expiration-time/e1a12c68d98384a8.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Modify the **Start date & time** and **End date & time **in the **Test Expiration Time** section.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/modify-test-expiration-time/17804d438bef6e75.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Save Changes**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/modify-test-expiration-time/240b906df6694e40.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
