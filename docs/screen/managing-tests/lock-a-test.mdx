Locking a test prevents changes to its questions and settings. Only test owners and company administrators can lock or unlock a test.

When a test is locked, you cannot:

- Edit test settings
- Change the order of questions
- Add new questions
- Modify existing questions

<Note>
**Note:** You cannot delete a locked test, but you can archive it.
</Note>

## Prerequisites

You must have an existing test in your HackerRank account.

## Locking a test

To lock a test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test you want to lock.
1. Click the more options menu (⋮) in the top right corner
1. Select **Lock**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/lock-a-test/52d2dbdb08262c2b.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Lock** to confirm. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/lock-a-test/41519f045f698cc3.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

## Unlocking a test

<Note>
**Note:** To make changes to a locked test, you must first unlock it.
</Note>

To unlock a test:

1. Open the test you want to unlock.
1. Click the more options menu (⋮) in the top right corner.
1. Select **Unlock**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/lock-a-test/efaf136aefdb0cd9.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. In the confirmation dialog, click **Unlock** to confirm. A success message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/lock-a-test/42feeb53e9e1ccfa.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
