The candidates list in the test interface provides a centralized view of all invited candidates and their test statuses. It helps you track candidate progress, review reports, and manage invitations in one place.

## Viewing the candidates list

To view the list of invited candidates for a specific test:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Tests** tab.
1. Open the test from which you want to view or manage candidate invitations.
1. Go to the **Candidates** tab to view the list of all candidates who have been invited or have participated in the test.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/view-candidates-list-for-a-test/49e009ff97ee8937.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Use the search bar to find candidates by their name or email address.
1. Apply filters from the left pane using the default options, or click **Add Filter** to create a custom filter.
