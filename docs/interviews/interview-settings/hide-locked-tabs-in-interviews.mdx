You can use the **Automatic Tab Locking and Hiding** feature to help candidates stay focused during an interview. This feature ensures that only the active tab is visible to the candidate, allowing you to control the interview flow.

## Key benefits

The **Automatic Tab Locking and Hiding** feature offers the following benefits:

- **Controlled access for interviewers**: Interviewers can restrict poorly performing candidates from accessing additional tabs. These tabs remain hidden from the candidate.
- **Improved candidate focus**: Candidates stay attentive without distractions from other tabs, including those the interviewer plans to use later in the interview.

<Note>
**Note:** The **Automatic Tab Locking and Hiding feature **is disabled by default.
</Note>

## Enable automatic tab lock and hide

To enable automatic tab lock and hide:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Select your **Profile** icon in the upper-right corner.
1. Select **Settings** from the drop-down menu.
1. In the left panel, select **Interview Settings** under the **Company** section.
1. Select the **Enable Automatic lock** checkbox.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/hide-locked-tabs-in-interviews/e4a7b48777d2a534.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click **Save**.

When enabled, this setting applies to all interviews in the account.

## Interviewer experience

After you enable the **Automatic Tab Locking and Hiding **feature and open an interview, the system locks all tabs added before or during the interview by default. The system hides these locked tabs from the candidate.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hide-locked-tabs-in-interviews/ebee9ec2a176b816.png" alt="image" />
</Frame>

To make a tab visible to the candidate, click **Unlock**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hide-locked-tabs-in-interviews/ed8dc8cddcbd8620.png" alt="image" />
</Frame>

## Candidate experience

When a candidate joins the interview, they see a blank screen until the interviewer unlocks a tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hide-locked-tabs-in-interviews/1676dc70e39b2fcc.png" alt="image" />
</Frame>

Once a question tab is unlocked, the candidate can view and interact with it.
