You can download the interview list as a `.csv` file to view, share, or analyze interview data offline.

## Prerequisite

You have created at least one interview. For more information, see [📄 Create an Interview](https://app.notion.com/docs/3147b674-5cbb-4bde-9528-7699bb64b1a7/articles/28cfbf56-d0f6-49be-a4b8-ebeda4c0f974).

## Exporting interview list

To export the interview list:

1. Log in to your HackerRank for Work account using your credentials.
1. Go to **Interviews** \> **Interview List**.
1. (Optional) Use the search bar or filters to find specific interviews. For more information, see [Search and filter interviews](https://support.hackerrank.com/articles/8404005945-creating-an-interview#search-and-filter-interviews).
1. Click the export icon.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/export-interview-list/9f3a0ebba7b9b1b0.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The platform downloads the interview list as a `.csv` file, and a success message appears.

<Note>
**Note:** If the download does not start automatically, click **Click here** in the **Download Interviews List** dialog box to download the file.
</Note>
