As a recruiter, you can schedule an interview between candidates and interviewers. You can start an interview immediately or schedule an interview at a convenient time.

## Starting an interview immediately

to start an interview immediately:

1. Log in to your HackerRank for Work account using your credentials.
1. Go to **Interviews** \> **Interview List**.
1. Click **New**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/create-an-interview/b1cded561f6ad2c0.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The system opens the interview interface.
1. Click **Invite.**
1. Copy the interview link and share it with the candidate.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/create-an-interview/1ef757d6e992504a.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  The candidate can use the link to join the interview immediately.

  <Note>
  **Note:** You can also generate a new interview link by selecting the dropdown next to **New**, then selecting **Generate link**. The system copies the link to your clipboard.
  </Note>

## Scheduling an interview

To schedule an interview:

1. Log in to your HackerRank for Work account using your credentials.
1. Go to **Interviews** \> **Interview List**.
1. Click the drop-down next to **New** and select **Schedule for later**.

</Note>

## Search and filter Interviews

To search and filter Interviews:

1. Go to **Interviews** **\> Interview List**. The page displays a list of all interviews you have created.
1. Use the search bar or filters to find specific interviews.

## Candidate experience

When candidates join an interview, HackerRank guides them through the same setup process as interviewers. The setup verifies their system compatibility.

After setup, the candidate enters the interview lobby.
