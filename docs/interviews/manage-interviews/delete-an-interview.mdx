You can delete a scheduled HackerRank Interview at any time. When you delete an interview, HackerRank automatically sends email notifications to the candidate and all interviewers.

## Prerequisites

You must have already created and scheduled an interview.

## Deleting an Interview

To delete a scheduled interview:

1. Log in to your **HackerRank for Work** account using your credentials.
1. Go to the **Interviews** \> **Interview List**. The page displays a list of all interviews you have created.
1. Click the more options menu (**⋮**) next to the interview you want to delete.
1. Click **Delete Interview**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-an-interview/1188a4a2622aef87.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>

  A confirmation message appears.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/delete-an-interview/20e45fdd2e4256c2.png" alt="image" />
  </Frame>
1. Click** Delete** to confirm.
